Mission

Why we exist

The shipping lanes built for the 20th century assumed a predictable world.

Routes were stable. Tariffs were rare. A container loaded in Yantian on a Tuesday could be costed, hedged, and scheduled with a spreadsheet. That world is gone.

The modern climate is geopolitical, climatic, and microsecond. Chokepoints close overnight. Tariff schedules are weaponized. A single weather system can strand a quarter of inventory three time zones away. Spreadsheets cannot price this.

Supply Vector is the alternative — a continuously-learning model of every node, lane, and supplier in your network, listening to the world in real time and re-solving your operations the moment the ground shifts. Not a dashboard. A nervous system.
