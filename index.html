FAQ

Common questions.

Everything from data sources to onboarding to security. If we missed yours, the team replies within a day.

How quickly can Supply Vector integrate with our existing ERP?
Most pilots are reading from SAP, Oracle, or NetSuite within a week. We bring read-only connectors for the common stacks and run alongside your current planners until you trust the recommendations.
What real-world data sources does the model use?
Customs and tariff schedules, NOAA and ECMWF weather, maritime AIS feeds, port congestion indices, geopolitical risk scores, plus the public-record health of every supplier and Tier-2 in your graph.
How do you weigh competing priorities like cost vs. emissions?
You set the weights — nine levers covering cost, speed, resilience, sustainability, ethics, tariffs, emissions, supplier risk, and geopolitics. The solver is multi-objective, so trade-offs are explicit rather than hidden in a black box.
Where does my data live, and who can see it?
SOC 2 Type II controls, optional single-tenant deployment in your cloud, and zero training on customer data. Every recommendation carries an audit-grade decision log so your finance and compliance teams can trace any change.
Do I need to commit to a full network redesign on day one?
No. Most teams start with a single lane or product family, validate the recommendations against historical disruptions, and expand only when the model has earned trust.
